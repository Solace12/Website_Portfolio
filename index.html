Digital Art

  • Galaxy head

    This is a surreal digital illustration that blends a human portrait with a cosmic scene. The head is a semi-dissolved and abstractly incomplete, with the upper portion removed to reveal a vibrant universe. This digital art creatively suggests that what’s inside the mind is more infinite and wondrous than what we can see on the outside.

  • Rooftop Silhouette

    This minimalist digital illustration shows a solitary figure sitting on a rooftop, gazing into a violet sky scattered with birds, clouds, and a softly glowing moon. Despite its simplicity, the piece carries a strong emotional undertone of solitude, contemplation, and serenity.

  • The Mask

    This artwork features a single theatrical mask split in half, placed against a starkly contrasting black and red background. On one side, the mask weeps, surrounded by broken hearts. On the other, the mask smiles faintly, surrounded by whole hearts. It's a raw visual representation of the duality of emotional experience most notably the split between pain and love, grief and joy, or inner suffering versus outer expression.
